About This Service

What is OpenClaw Consulting?

OpenClaw consulting is not a templated service. Every engagement starts with a deep discovery call where I learn about your business, your pain points, and your goals. I don't sell you what I have. I build what you need.

What you get:

  • Discovery & Strategy — Understanding your specific workflow and identifying AI automation opportunities
  • Custom Setup — Installation on your Mac Mini or VPS, configured for your exact use case
  • Training & Documentation — Hands-on training so you can manage and extend the system yourself
  • Security Hardening — Prompt injection defense, permission controls, and data protection from day one
  • 30-Day Support — Direct access for questions, tweaks, and optimization

Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does implementation take?

Setup is typically 5-7 business days from start to finish. This includes discovery, setup, configuration, and initial training. Some complex integrations may take longer.

What happens after the initial 30 days?

You own the system entirely. You can run it yourself, or I offer ongoing optimization and maintenance packages if you prefer hands-off management.

What if I'm not technical?

That's exactly who this is for. I handle all the technical complexity. You interact with your AI assistant through natural conversation on Telegram, WhatsApp, or Discord.

Can you integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. OpenClaw integrates with Notion, CRMs, email, Slack, Google Workspace, custom APIs, and more. We'll map your specific toolchain during discovery.
